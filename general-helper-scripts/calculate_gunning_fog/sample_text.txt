This script calculates the Gunning fog index. This index is a readability test for English writing. The index estimates the years of formal education a person needs to understand the text on the first reading.

A lower score indicates easier readability. A score around 12 is considered ideal for most general-purpose writing. Scores above 17 are very difficult.

This particular implementation is not overly complicated. It parses text quantitatively to determine sentence length and identify complex polysyllabic words, which are fundamental components of the calculation. This provides a comprehensive readability approximation.
